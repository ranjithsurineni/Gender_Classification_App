Welcome to End-to-End Face Recognition Web App


This web application enables users to upload images for face recognition. It leverages deep learning models to detect and identify faces in real time. Whether for security, authentication, or research purposes, our face recognition system ensures accuracy and efficiency.

How It Works:

  • Step 1: Upload an image with one or multiple faces.
  • Step 2: The AI model processes the image and detects faces.
  • Step 3: Identified faces are displayed with their respective labels or IDs.
  • Step 4: Users can analyze results or export them for further use.

Features:

  • Real-time face detection and recognition
  • Supports multiple faces in a single image
  • High accuracy with deep learning models
  • Simple and intuitive user interface
